About

I am a full stack web developer in development in the San Francisco Bay Area. Using technology in my classroom piqued my interest in web development, particularly with how young people interact with learning technologies. I intend to use my web development skills to help bridge the tech learning gap for students in underserved communities and foster opportunities for collaboration between tech companies and those very same students.


When I'm not thinking about young people and technology I am often rooting for FC Liverpool and the Michigan Wolverines. Go Blue!
